About the Coach

Meet Sanjay Kumar

A dedicated coach who believes every stroke counts — and every swimmer has a champion inside.

Coaching at Baroda Swimfront

Hi, I'm Sanjay Kumar — Head Coach at Baroda Swimfront and a lifelong lover of the water. With over 5 years of hands-on coaching experience, I've had the privilege of guiding swimmers of all ages — from nervous beginners taking their first dip, to seasoned athletes refining their competitive edge.

My coaching philosophy is simple: swimming is a life skill, not just a sport. I focus on water safety, building confidence, and developing real technique — all wrapped in a warm, encouraging environment where learners actually enjoy showing up.

Holding a National Certificate in Swimming, I bring both the credentials and the passion to make every session count. Whether you're 7 or 70, there's a place for you in my pool.

What I Teach

My Specialities

From mastering the perfect butterfly to building endurance for open water — here's where I shine.

🏊
Freestyle

The foundation of swimming. I help you build speed, efficiency, and proper breathing rhythm from the ground up.

🤽
Backstroke

Master the relaxed, rhythmic backstroke — great for posture, coordination, and back-muscle development.

🌊
Breaststroke

A classic, elegant stroke perfect for endurance and relaxation. I focus on timing and form for maximum efficiency.

🦋
Butterfly

The most powerful stroke in swimming. I break it down into achievable steps so you actually enjoy learning it.

🌅
Open Water Training

Prepare for lakes, rivers, and open-water events with safety-first techniques and environmental awareness.

🏅
Competitive Coaching

From beginner to competitive level — race strategy, turns, starts, and performance optimization for meets.

💪
Fitness Swimming

Low-impact, full-body conditioning in the water. Perfect for weight management, cardio, and overall wellness.

🧓
Senior Citizen Training

Specially adapted programs for seniors — gentle, safe, and deeply beneficial for joints, balance, and mental health.

🩺
Rehabilitation Swimming

Therapeutic aquatic sessions designed in collaboration with recovery goals — ideal post-injury or post-surgery.
